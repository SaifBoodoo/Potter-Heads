Gryffindor Information

With the lion as its symbol this is a house that values courage and chivalry while being drawn towards a sense of heroism. Members of this house tend to have a strong moral compass leaning towards excelling in acts they deem as good. They however also tend to be hot headed as they can be blinded by bravery and daringness. These traits can also cause excessive arrogance and recklessness.



Founder: Godric Gryffindor




House Colours: Red and Gold




Notable Members: Harry Potter,Ron Weasley, Hermione Granger, Albus Dumbledore, Neville Longbottom, Minerva McGonnogall
