Ravenclaw Information

With the eagle as its symbol this is a house that prioritizes intelligence and curiosity. Members of this hosue are creatively inclined and are on a constant journey to discover new and out of the box ideas which leads to their strong sense of individuality. These traits can lead to a strong sense of isolation as they focus on discovery often leaving those around them in pursuit of knowledge or as a by product of built up arrogance.



Founder: Rowena Ravenclaw




House Colours: Blue and Bronze




Notable Members: Luna Lovegood,Cho Chang,Gilderoy Lockhart and Filius Flitwick
